CRACKJACK - Sound Effects Folder
================================

Add your sound effect files here!

Required files (any format):
- card-deal.mp3/wav/ogg  - Sound when a card is dealt
- card-flip.mp3/wav/ogg  - Sound when dealer reveals hidden card
- win.mp3/wav/ogg        - Sound when player wins
- lose.mp3/wav/ogg       - Sound when player loses
- chip.mp3/wav/ogg       - Sound when placing bets
- click.mp3/wav/ogg      - Sound when any button is clicked
- shuffle.mp3/wav/ogg    - Sound when deck is being shuffled

Supported formats: MP3, WAV, OGG

Tips:
- Keep sounds short (under 1 second for most sounds)
- Card deal: A quick "whoosh" or "slap" sound
- Card flip: A satisfying "flip" or "snap" sound  
- Win: A cheerful jingle or cash register sound
- Lose: A sad trombone or buzzer sound
- Chip: Casino chip clicking sound
- Click: A satisfying button click/pop sound
- Shuffle: A card shuffling/riffling sound (can be longer, ~1-2 seconds)

You can toggle SFX on/off in the Options menu!
